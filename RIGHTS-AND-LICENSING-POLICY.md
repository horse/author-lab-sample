# Rights and Licensing Policy

<!-- 这是一个 sample，文件实质完成后删掉这行注释 -->

No permission to reuse a source work is implied by its presence in a manifest. Each source record must declare its rights status and storage policy. Copyright-restricted EPUB, PDF, image, audio, correspondence, and archival files should remain in authorized private storage unless redistribution rights are documented.

Generated code, research notes, author models, and publications may require different licenses. Do not add a repository-wide license until those categories have been reviewed separately.
