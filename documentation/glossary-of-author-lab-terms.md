# Glossary of Author-Lab Terms

<!-- 这是一个 sample，文件实质完成后删掉这行注释 -->

- **Source author:** the real author whose materials are researched.
- **Source corpus:** primary and secondary materials about the source author.
- **Source-author model:** a compact, versioned interpretation compiled from accepted research.
- **Derived author:** a pseudonymous editorial persona created through explicit transformation.
- **Derivation profile:** the record of inheritance, transformation, rejection, and original additions.
- **Harness:** shared execution contracts, stages, quality gates, and templates.
- **Runbook:** one concrete workflow for a writing task type.
- **Runtime adapter:** environment-specific model and tool configuration.
- **Work item:** a self-contained writing task with state and artifacts.
